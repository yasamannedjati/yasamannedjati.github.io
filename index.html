About

I’m interested in design as a form of research and a way of understanding how people relate to space. My work often revolves around perceptions of isolation within urban environments, and the role narrative and scenography can play in shaping spatial experiences. I tend to approach projects through a multidisciplinary process that treats design as a way of thinking, questioning, and making sense of the world.
